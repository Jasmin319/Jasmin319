# Hi, I’m Jasmin 👋

I work at the intersection of data, structure, and decision-making.

My focus is on Power BI, data governance, and AI with Copilot and Copilot Studio.

I don’t see BI as reporting or AI as a feature.  
I see both as systems that need structure, context, and clarity to actually work.

As a Microsoft MVP and freelance BI consultant, I build solutions that are not only technically correct — but understandable, maintainable, and usable in real business contexts.

---

## What I focus on

- building structured and scalable Power BI models  
- designing KPIs and dashboards that support decisions  
- improving data quality and shared understanding  
- creating reusable BI patterns and workflows  
- integrating AI (Copilot & Copilot Studio) into real processes
